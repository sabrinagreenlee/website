Projects

Tax Centers Map

I am currently working with GeorgiaWatch.org to create a feature on their current website using MapBox. It is for low-income people and their care-givers to search and find tax centers they can utilize for free. This project aims to reduce victims of predatory tax refund loans.

Recruitment Allocation Visualization Tool

I worked with Georgia Tech’s Institutional Research Services to design an interactive visualization tool. The department wanted to know which high schools in Georgia they should concentrate allocating their recruiters. This project dealt with Georgia Tech’s student data for the past 10 years. D3.js was mainly used for the implementation.

IOS APP UI Design

The app was for facilitating food gatherings such as potlucks. I got to experience the full iterative process of UI design with this project. It involved getting requirements via surveys and interviews, making prototypes, and conducting heuristic evaluations and usability tests.
